Sep 2018 - Present: Data Analytics and Society CDT Student at The University of Manchester School of Social Sciences

Data Analytics and Society is an ESRC funded Centre for Doctoral Training. I am pursuing a 4 year integrated PhD and MSc in Data Analytics and Society with The University of Manchester as my home institution. My research will focus on developing data analytics techniques to simulate and understand the development of the Greater Manchester Metropolitan Area. The taught element of the programme is designed to provide students with the foundational skills to carry out research, modules include:

  • Supporting startups on the Geovation Accelerator Programme through bespoke Data Science solutions to empower them to maximise the value of their data

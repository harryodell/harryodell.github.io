Employment

Sep 2018 - Present: Data Analytics and Society CDT Student @ The University of Manchester School of Social Sciences

Data Analytics and Society is an ESRC funded Centre for Doctoral Training. I am pursuing a 4 year integrated PhD and MSc in Data Analytics and Society with The University of Manchester as my home institution. My research will focus on developing data analytics techniques to simulate and understand the development of the Greater Manchester Metropolitan Area. Greater Manchester Combined Authority are an external partner on the project. The taught element of the programme is designed to provide students with the foundational skills to carry out our research, modules include:

  • Programming for Social Sciences – University of Leeds
  • Analysis of Human Dynamics – University of Liverpool
  • Understanding Data and its Environment – University of Manchester
  • Social Analytics & Visualisation – University of Sheffield
